# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1.

## TL;DR

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good faith.
- No harassment, personal attacks, or dogpiling.
- No spam or off-topic self-promotion.
- Maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributors who violate the above.

## Reporting

Send reports to **conduct@roninforge.org**. Reports are confidential and will be acted on within a reasonable timeframe.
